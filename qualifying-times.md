These are the current qualifying times for the City Meet (Championship Meet). Swimmers must achieve these times during the regular season to qualify. These standards are in effect for the **2026 and 2027 seasons**.

## Notes

- **Age determination:** Swimmer's age as of June 1st of the current season
- **Combined age groups:** Some events (8&U Breaststroke/Butterfly, 10&U IM) combine multiple age groups
- **Standards effective:** 2026 and 2027 seasons; scheduled for re-evaluation before 2028
